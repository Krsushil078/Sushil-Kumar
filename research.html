Research

Publications & Current Work

My research focuses on the valuation of ecosystem services, pollution impacts, and household behavior in environmental decision-making. I explore topics such as:

  • Valuation of ecosystem services using choice experiments
  • Health costs of arsenic contamination in groundwater
  • Behavioral economics related to water safety practices in rural India

Publications include peer-reviewed work in Groundwater for Sustainable Development and presentations at AERE, INSEE, and As2024 conferences.
