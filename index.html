About Me

Environmental Economist | Researcher | Educator

I am Sushil Kumar, currently pursuing a PhD in Environmental Economics at IIT Roorkee. I specialize in sustainability, resource management, and development economics. I aim to contribute to impactful research and teaching in environmental policy and econometrics.

Academic Background

PhD (ongoing) at IIT Roorkee, MA and BA in Economics from Patna University. Qualified UGC-NET and awarded JRF in 2016.

Research

Publications and Ongoing Work

My work focuses on the valuation of ecosystem services, health costs of arsenic contamination, and household behavior in environmental decision-making. Published in Groundwater for Sustainable Development and presented at major conferences like INSEE, As2024, and AERE.

Highlighted Publications

- Kumar & Nayak (2024, 2025), Groundwater for Sustainable Development
- Kaur & Kumar (2017), Journal of Economics, Patna University

Projects

Research & Teaching Experience

I've coordinated academic lecture series, served as a Teaching Assistant for NPTEL and departmental courses, and contributed to government-funded projects on ecological damage, tribal development, and fintech for social upliftment.
